## 1. Wallets & Discovery

**Files changed**: `nansen/wallet-discovery.ts`, `pumpfun/alpha-discovery.ts`, `pumpfun/graduation-discovery.ts` (NEW), `pumpfun/movers-tracker.ts` (NEW), `config/index.ts`, `wallets.json` (NEW), `scripts/import-wallets.ts` (NEW), `scripts/query-wallets.ts` (NEW)

### Key Changes

#### Seed Wallets Externalized (19 Mar)
- Moved hardcoded seed wallets out of source code into `wallets.json` (25 wallets).
- Added `import-wallets.ts` bulk import script and `query-wallets.ts` diagnostic script.
- Config now loads wallets dynamically via `loadSeedWallets()` from `wallets.json`.

#### Wallet Quality Filtering Overhaul (19-20 Mar)
- Seeds **no longer get a free pass** — they go through the same quality scoring as discovered wallets.
- Nansen data is boosted in wallet scoring (Nansen PnL/ROI/trade-count weighted higher).
- Signal scorer integrates Nansen realized PnL and holding ratio into score calculation.
- Stale bag-holder wallets are pruned automatically.
- **Deployer wallets are now blocked** — detected via DexScreener deployer field.

#### WalletSlotManager (20 Mar)
- New `WalletSlotManager` concept manages WS subscription slots.
- Quality-based rotation: lower-performing wallets get evicted when better ones are discovered.
- Tier-based slot counts: MICRO=15, SMALL=35, MEDIUM=20, FULL=30. Shadow mode=75.
- Ranking cache and shadow mode support added.

#### Discovery Sources Expanded (20-21 Mar)
- **Smart money DEX trader discovery**: discovers wallets with high win rates on DEX trades (via Nansen screener).
- **Pump.fun alpha discovery**: relaxed thresholds, higher caps — discovers more wallets from on-curve buys.
- **Graduation retroanalysis pipeline**: analyzes historically graduated pump.fun tokens, extracts top-performing early buyers, seeds them as wallets.
- Graduation analyzer added at `analysis/graduation-analyzer.ts`.
- Discovery interval shortened from 30min to 15min.

#### Movers Velocity Tracker (18 Mar — NEW)
- `pumpfun/movers-tracker.ts`: Detects high-momentum tokens from PumpPortal trade stream.
- Tracks SOL/min velocity per token, surfaces tokens accelerating toward graduation.
- Integrated with PumpPortal WebSocket client for real-time data.

#### Graduation Bounce Discovery (20 Mar — NEW)
- `pumpfun/graduation-discovery.ts`: Monitors freshly graduated pump.fun tokens for post-graduation dip/recovery pattern.
- Thesis: tokens dump 30-60% after graduation, then bounce 50-200%.
- Full config in `graduationDiscovery` block (see config snapshot below).
- `pumpfun/graduated-tracker.ts`: Tracks and manages graduation bounce positions.

## 2. Strategy & Trading (Pump.fun)

**Files changed**: `pumpfun/tracker.ts`, `pumpfun/validation.ts`, `config/index.ts`, `signals/entry-engine.ts`

### Fundamental Strategy Shift (18 Mar)
- **Deferred entry**: No longer entering at the alpha wallet's entry point. Now waits for the token to reach **28-38% bonding curve fill** (82% win rate zone in backtests).
- Entry window: `curveEntryMin: 0.33` (33%), `curveEntryMax: 0.38` (38%).
- Data showed 0-30% curve = death zone (-4.38 SOL net loss).

### Config Tuning from 499/538-Trade Datasets (19-20 Mar)
- **Conviction threshold raised**: `minConvictionSol` from 0.5 → 1.25 SOL (tiny buys = spray/noise).
- **Curve velocity gate**: `curveVelocityMin: 0.5` SOL/min (filter stalling tokens).
- **Profit target**: `profitTarget: 0.10` (10% PnL), `curveProfitTarget: 0.36` (sell at 36% curve fill).
- **Stop loss tightened**: `stopLoss: -0.10` (10%), `hardKill: -0.15` (15%).
- **Stale time kill**: `staleTimeKillMins: 0.75` (45 seconds — stalls are dead weight).
- **Max positions reduced**: `maxPositions: 2` (was 3 — focus capital).
- **Slippage reduced**: `slippageBps: 400` (was 500 — 5% was eating entire TP profit).
- **Token age cap**: `maxTokenAgeMins: 10` (curve scalps resolve in 2-5 min).
- **Deferred entry disabled**: After 8 trades with 0% WR and -0.83 SOL, deferred entries are turned off.
- **100% graduation sell**: `graduationSellPct: 100` (no lottery holds).

### Graduation Bounce Strategy (20 Mar — NEW)
- Separate position management for post-graduation dip/recovery plays.
- Entry: wait for 25% dip + 15% recovery + 2 stable price checks + 45% buy ratio.
- Position sizing: 80% of normal (higher risk).
- TP: 50%, SL: -20%, Hard kill: -30%, trailing stop at +25% (trail 15%).
- Max hold: 4 hours. Stale cut: 30 min.

### Bug Fixes (18-20 Mar)
- **Instant close bug**: Positions were being immediately closed due to stale initial price data. Fixed by using actual entry price.
- **Instant false stop-losses**: SL was firing on winning positions due to PnL calculation using stale cached price. Fixed.
- **Spike protection deadlock**: During rug pulls, spike protection was preventing sells, causing -99% losses. Fixed by allowing sells through during spikes.
- **Duplicate PnL/exit logic**: WebSocket handler had conflicting exit logic with tracker. Consolidated.

## 3. Strategy & Trading (DEX / Standard)

**Files changed**: `signals/entry-engine.ts`, `signals/signal-scorer.ts`, `config/index.ts`, `validation/gate.ts`

### Signal Scoring Changes (19-20 Mar)
- `minSignalScore` lowered to **20** for MICRO/SMALL tiers (was 35-45) — wider net for data collection.
- Shadow mode `minSignalScore`: **15** (max data collection).
- Nansen stats (PnL, ROI, trade count) now weighted higher in signal score calculation.
- Soft floor relaxed to reduce zero-signal periods.

### DEX Trade Gates Opened (20 Mar)
- `minCapitalForStandardTrading` lowered from 5 SOL to **0.5 SOL** — DEX trades needed for data even at low capital.
- MCap ranges widened: MICRO min $10K (was $30K), SMALL min $15K (was $50K), max $15M.
- Liquidity minimums lowered: MICRO $2K, SMALL $3K.
- Momentum range widened: -60% to +500% (was -50% to +300%).
- Volume multiplier min: 0.5x (was 1x).
- Token age limits removed (`tokenMaxAgeDays: null`).

### Validation Gate
- DexScreener data now **required** — tokens without DexScreener data are blocked (`NO_DEX_DATA` validation result).
- Deployer wallet detection added to validation.

### Re-entry Logic (20 Mar — NEW)
- Conditional re-entry into previously closed tokens.
- Cooldown: 15 min (MICRO/SMALL), 30 min (MEDIUM/FULL).
- Max 1 re-entry per token per session.
- Re-entry size: 60% of normal (MICRO/SMALL), 50% (MEDIUM/FULL).
- `BlockedTokenMeta` type tracks exit context for re-entry decisions.

## 4. Portfolio & Position Management

**Files changed**: `positions/live-tracker.ts`, `positions/shadow-tracker.ts`, `trading/capital-manager.ts`, `types/index.ts`

### Momentum-Based Hold Time Extension (20 Mar — NEW)
- When a time-kill window approaches, if the token still has positive momentum, the hold time is extended.
- Configurable per tier: extension hours, max extensions per window, minimum PnL to qualify.
- MICRO/SMALL: 1h extension, max 1 per window, min PnL -2%.
- MEDIUM/FULL: 2h extension, max 2 per window, must be breakeven+.
- Tracked via `momentum_extensions` map on Position/ShadowPosition types.

### Stop-Loss Hold-Time Guard (21 Mar — NEW)
- `stopLossGuardMins: 3` — suppresses SL (not hard kill) for first 3 minutes after entry.
- Protects against early volatility dips that would trigger false SL exits.
- Only applies to MICRO/SMALL tiers (established token tiers don't need it).

### Dynamic SL Widening for Micro-Cap Tokens (21 Mar — NEW)
- `stopLossWidenMcapThreshold: 100_000` — tokens under $100K mcap get wider stop-loss.
- `stopLossWidenFactor: 1.5` — e.g., -20% SL becomes -30% for micro-cap tokens.
- Disabled for MEDIUM/FULL tiers.

### Position Sizing
- Pump.fun multiplier: 1.20x (120% of normal tier sizing).
- Graduation bounce multiplier: 0.80x (80% of normal).
- MICRO/SMALL tier maxPositions increased to 5 (was 3).
- `minPositionSol` lowered for SMALL: 0.15 SOL (was 0.3).

### Types Added
- `BlockedTokenMeta` interface for re-entry tracking.
- `momentum_extensions` and `reentry_count` fields on Position and ShadowPosition.
- `stopLossGuardMins`, `stopLossWidenMcapThreshold`, `stopLossWidenFactor` on TierConfig.
- `WalletSource` enum expanded: `GRADUATION_SEED`, `GRADUATION_DISCOVERY`.
- `ValidationResult.NO_DEX_DATA` added.
